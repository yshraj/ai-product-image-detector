    Privacy Policy

    RealModel Filter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us. There is no account, no server, and no analytics or tracking.

    What stays on your device

    Your preferences (display mode, thresholds, active sites) sync only across your own browser profile via Chrome Sync. Detection results are cached in your browser's local storage. Your Hugging Face access token is stored locally and is never shared with us.

    What leaves your device

    When you connect Hugging Face, product image bytes and your token are sent only to Hugging Face's API to classify the image, governed by Hugging Face's own privacy policy. With the on-device Preview engine, nothing leaves your browser.

    Permissions

    Host access is limited to the supported marketplaces, their image CDNs, and the Hugging Face API. The extension only fetches public image URLs.

    Terms of Use

    RealModel Filter is provided "as is", without warranty of any kind. It is a decision-support tool, not a guarantee.

    Accuracy

    No AI-image detector is perfect. The Preview heuristic is explicitly low-accuracy; even connected models can mislabel images. Do not rely on it as the sole basis for any purchasing or legal decision.

    Third-party services

    Using the Hugging Face engine is subject to Hugging Face's terms and rate limits. You are responsible for your own access token and its usage.
