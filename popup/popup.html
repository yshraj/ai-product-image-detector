How to get a free key
  1. Open huggingface.co/join and create a free account.
  2. Go to Settings → Access Tokens.
  3. Click New token, role Read, name it RealModel, create it.
  4. Copy the token (starts with hf_) and paste it above.
  5. Press Connect. The first scan can take ~20s while the model warms up.
